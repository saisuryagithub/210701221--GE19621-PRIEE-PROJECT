Glossary of Terms

Discover the rich vocabulary of Hindu scriptures, temples, and architecture:

Architectural Terms

Gopuram
The monumental, ornate tower at the entrance of a South Indian temple.
Vimana
The tower-like structure above the main shrine (garbhagriha) of a Hindu temple.

Terms Related to Scriptures

Vedas
The most ancient and sacred scriptures of Hinduism, a collection of hymns, rituals and philosophy.
Upanishads
Philosophical texts forming the later part of the Vedas, focusing on concepts of Brahman and Atman.
